First fact

Zebras are actually black with white stripes.

Second fact

Fennec fox is the smallest fox in the world. Their feet are very hairy, which protect them from extremely hot sand.

Third fact

Fourth fact

Tufted deers are most active at dusk or dawn. They also have two big front fangs.

Fifth fact

Komodo dragon is the largest lizard on earth. It is also super venomous.

Sixth fact

Eighth fact
